Dosa

Description

Dosa is a famous south Indian Dish. it goes well with groundnut chutney

Ingredients

  • Dosa Batter
  • Oil
  • Karam powder

Steps

  1. Mix the batter well
  2. Spread the batter well on a flat pan
  3. Add little oil
  4. Once it's cooked spread karam powder on the Dosa
  5. Yummy Dosa is prepared
