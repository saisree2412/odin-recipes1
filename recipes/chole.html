Chole

Description

Chole is a popular Punjabi dish served best with Puri also popularly called as Bhature

Ingredients

  • Chick Peas
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Biryani spices
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Chole Masala
  • Salt

Steps

  1. soak the chickpeas overnight
  2. Boil the chickpeas in a cooker until you get 4 whistles
  3. In a separate pan add oil,butter,jeera and biryani spices
  4. add onion paste and tomato psate and ginger garlic paste
  5. Once oil gets seperated from the above mixture add salt
  6. Add the cooked chickpeas and close the lid and cook for 15mins
  7. Yummy Chole is Ready!!
